## STEP 10: Identity Verification (Individual Accounts)

### For Individual Accounts Only:

Apple may ask you to verify your identity with a government-issued ID.

**You'll Receive:**
- Email with subject: "Verify your identity for the Apple Developer Program"
- Link to upload ID

**What to Do:**
1. Click link in email
2. Choose ID type: Driver's License, Passport, or State ID
3. Take a clear photo of your ID:
   - Front of ID clearly visible
   - All text readable
   - No glare or shadows
   - Good lighting
4. Upload photo
5. Submit for review

**Tips:**
- Use good lighting
- Ensure name matches your Apple ID exactly
- ID must be current (not expired)
- Clear, high-resolution photo

**Processing Time:**
- Usually reviewed within a few hours
- Can take up to 24 hours

## STEP 11: D-U-N-S Number Verification (Organization Accounts)

### For Organization Accounts Only:

Apple will verify your D-U-N-S number and organization information.

**What Happens:**
1. Apple looks up your D-U-N-S number in Dun & Bradstreet database
2. Confirms organization name matches
3. May contact you for additional verification
4. May ask for legal documents (Articles of Incorporation, etc.)

**Timeline:**
- Automated verification: 1-2 business days
- Manual verification (if needed): 1-2 weeks

**You May Receive:**
- Email requesting additional documentation
- Phone call from Apple Developer Support (from Cupertino, CA area code)
- Request to verify you're authorized to enroll

**Be Responsive:**
- Answer emails within 24 hours
- Answer phone calls if they ring
- Provide requested documents promptly

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Issue 1: Payment Declined

**Symptoms:** Credit card rejected during purchase

**Causes:**
- Insufficient funds
- Card doesn't support international transactions
- Billing address mismatch
- Bank fraud protection triggered

**Solutions:**
1. Contact your bank to authorize Apple charges
2. Use different payment method
3. Ensure billing address matches card exactly
4. Try again in a few hours (temporary bank hold)

### Issue 2: Name Doesn't Match ID

**Symptoms:** Identity verification fails

**Causes:**
- Apple ID name doesn't match government ID
- Middle name or initial missing/extra
- Hyphenated name formatted differently
- Legal name change not updated

**Solutions:**
1. Update Apple ID name to match ID exactly: https://appleid.apple.com
2. Wait 24 hours for changes to sync
3. Re-upload ID photo
4. Contact Apple Developer Support if issues persist

### Issue 3: D-U-N-S Number Not Found (Organizations)

**Symptoms:** "We couldn't find your organization" error

**Causes:**
- D-U-N-S number not yet in database
- Typo in D-U-N-S number entry
- Organization name doesn't match Dun & Bradstreet exactly
- D-U-N-S recently issued (not yet synced)

**Solutions:**
1. Verify D-U-N-S number at https://www.dnb.com/duns-number/lookup.html
2. Check organization name matches exactly
3. Wait 24-48 hours if D-U-N-S just issued
4. Contact Dun & Bradstreet to verify listing

### Issue 4: Organization Verification Delayed

**Symptoms:** No response after 2 weeks

**Causes:**
- Additional documentation needed
- Apple unable to verify legal authority
- Website not accessible or doesn't match
- Business email domain mismatch

**Solutions:**
1. Check spam folder for Apple emails
2. Call Apple Developer Support: 1-800-633-2152
3. Prepare documents: Articles of Incorporation, EIN letter
4. Ensure website is live and matches domain email

### Issue 5: Two-Factor Authentication Issues

**Symptoms:** Can't receive 2FA codes

**Causes:**
- Phone number changed
- No trusted device available
- Lost access to trusted phone

**Solutions:**
1. Use account recovery: https://iforgot.apple.com
2. Generate recovery key at https://appleid.apple.com
3. Add additional trusted phone number
4. Contact Apple Support: 1-800-692-7753
